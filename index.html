About Me

As a UI/UX designer with a focus on UI/UX, I am passionate about creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites and software. With Figma as my go-to tool, I have honed my skills in designing responsive interfaces that provide an exceptional user experience. My strengths include responsive design, user experience, and accessibility. I am also well-versed in creating wireframes and prototypes that help me visualize the final product and make necessary changes before the development phase.

Design Process

How I work

Strategy

Before starting the designing, I always make sure that I am asking the right questions and trying to accomplish the right challenges. This stage includes goals, research, analysis, and solutions.

Design

A problem solving phase. At the end, you will receive a pixel perfect design for your product. This stage includes wireframes, mockups, and prototypes simulating final end results before development.

Development

Since I'm a designer who can code too, I can easily work in close collaboration with developers until the product gets a life. In case of web projects I also do front-end development.

What Services Do I Offer

I create user-friendly and attractive digital products that meet the needs and expectations of my clients and users. Some of the services I do are:

User Testing

I evaluate the usability and desirability of your product by testing it with real or potential users. I also collect and analyze feedback, metrics, and data to identify and fix any issues or areas of improvement.

UX Consulting

I provide advice and guidance to clients on how to improve the user experience of your existing or new products. I also communicate and collaborate with other stakeholders, such as developers, marketers, and managers, to ensure that the design solutions align with your business goals and user needs.

UI UX Design

I design the visual elements of your product, such as colors, fonts, icons, and animations. I also create mockups, prototypes, and style guides to demonstrate how your product looks and feels.
